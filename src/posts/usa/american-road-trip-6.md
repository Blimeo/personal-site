**Day 14: Las Vegas -> Newport Beach**

</div>
The drive from Las Vegas to the greater Los Angeles area takes you straight through the scorching Mojave Desert, passing through towns like Barstow whose economies are built upon being oversized restrooms. But there is light at the end of the tunnel. 
<Image src="https://images.blimeo.dev/usa-road-trip/IMG_5584_00.jpeg"/>
Newport Beach is an affluent beach town in Orange County, an hour's drive from LA proper. We rented bikes and toured the peninsula which contains a marina and beautiful homes.
